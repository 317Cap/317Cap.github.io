Summerlyn

Premium Living Experience: Summerlyn Apartments provide a total of 152,000 square feet of rentable living area, along with an array of amenities including tennis courts, a basketball court, a pool, and a fitness center. This investment is designed to offer a premium living experience for residents.

Strategic Location: The apartments are strategically located near the Fort Hood Military Base, where there is a 3-month housing waiting list. The proximity to this significant demand generator enhances the investment's appeal.

Value Enhancement Plan: The plan to increase cash flow and property value by 15% includes a comprehensive renovation of all 200 units to ensure they compete with comparable properties. Additionally, exterior updates to facades and common areas will elevate the property's visual appeal. The implementation of a new property management firm, known for superior market performance, is part of the strategy.
