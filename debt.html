Private Loans

We offer interest-only loans, typically ranging from $1 million to $5 million. Characteristics of our loans include:

  • 6-12 months in length
  • Built-in extensions for the borrower
  • 90 days of guaranteed interest, no other prepayment penalties
  • Rates ranging from 10%-14%
  • 2-4 points of origination
  • Quick closing, usually 14-30 days
  • Open to creative lending scenarios
  • 60% LTV

*The duration and IRR for current loans were calculated as of December 31, 2024. Changing market conditions may impact terms.
