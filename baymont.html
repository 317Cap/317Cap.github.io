Baymont

Baymont is a hotel property that has been part of 317 Capital Group's portfolio.

Location and Accessibility: Strategically positioned in Murray, Utah, right next to I-15, the Baymont Project enjoys high visibility and central access. With convenient access to public transit, grocery stores, and other essential amenities, this location offers both convenience and connectivity.

Quick to Market: A light rehab and a 12-14 month lease-up plan ensure that this visionary project becomes a reality in no time.

Total Deal Cycle: With an 18-month targeted total deal cycle, investors can anticipate seeing returns relatively quickly, thanks to the efficient execution of this redevelopment.

Affordable Living in High Demand: The demand for affordable housing has never been higher. The Baymont Project addresses this pressing need, providing a community of affordable for-rent units that will make a meaningful impact on the local housing landscape.
