Kaysville OPS

Prominent Location: Kaysville OPS enjoys an enviable location right on 200 North in Kaysville, Utah, offering high visibility in the heart of the community. This prime location provides residents with easy access to a bustling retail center, where they can find everything from groceries at Smith's to delectable treats at Kneaders Bakery, energizing workouts at Orange Theory Fitness, and a quick bite at Jimmy John's or a caffeine fix at Starbucks.

Connectivity and Convenience: With just a 2-minute drive to I-15, Kaysville OPS offers exceptional connectivity to neighboring areas, making it an ideal choice for those seeking both urban amenities and suburban tranquility. Additionally, the development will directly connect to the Rail Trail walking and biking trail, promoting an active and outdoor-focused lifestyle.
